We looked at donations by month and watched how the donation amounts changed over the full years of data. First, this is the entire life of data, and the amounts of donations tracked by month number. We see a solid base (highly concentrated) of lower amounts and a straggling of higher-dollar donations.

A closer look at the time progression over the five full years shows that there was a trend of consolidation: high-ticket donations peter out as the foundation grows stronger. Hypothesis: target donors with large giving potential.
